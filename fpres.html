DoS attack - Signal jamming

Introduction

I used Huawei Matebook with AMD Ryzen 5 processor and Kali Linux 64-bit operating system installed in baremetal. Tool for jamming the signal I used Aircrack-ng.

Steps

  • 1. I changed the wireless interface to monitor mode
  • 2. I listened broadcast messages of APs that are near by me and chose the "victim" (my own device)
  • 3. Implement a deauthentication attack.

Final words

Wireless access points works in hafl-duplex mode and sends broadcast messages
